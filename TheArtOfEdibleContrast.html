The Art of Edible Contrast

Welcome to my school project, The Art of Edible contrast, a magazine concept centered around food photography that celebrates vibrant, contrasting colors and the art of culinary presentation. This project was designed to capture the rich textures, bold tones, and striking visuals that make food not only a pleasure to taste but a feast for the eyes.

Throughout the magazine, I aimed to create compositions that emphasize color contrast, bringing out the natural beauty of each dish. While not all the photographs in this project are mine, I selected and arranged each piece to tell a cohesive story. You can find more of my personal food photography style in my project, "Vivid Bites," where I further explore dynamic colors.

Hope you enjoy this flavorful visual journey!

Abria

This project showcases the creation of a branding and packaging concept for a new line of craft beers inspired by the rich cultural and natural heritage of Cantabria.

The work includes a deep dive into market research, naming development, visual identity design, and compliance with technical labeling standards. The result is a cohesive and visually striking product line that balances tradition and modernity, appealing to both local and broader audiences. Through careful attention to detail, this project demonstrates my ability to merge storytelling, design, and strategy to create an engaging and market-ready product concept.

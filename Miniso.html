Miniso

This project involved designing a 3D window display for Miniso, featuring their Sanrio collection with a festive Christmas theme. The goal was to create a visually engaging and cheerful display that captured the playful essence of the brand while celebrating the holiday season.

This project allowed me to explore 3D modeling, color theory, and spatial composition, resulting in a display that blends creativity with brand storytelling.

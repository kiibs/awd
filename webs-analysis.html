MANA

Mana Yerba Mate is an online platform dedicated to promoting the natural benefits of yerba mate, while also providing an e-commerce section where customers can explore and purchase a variety of products.

Reasons why I like the website:

  • User Experience (UX): The site features an intuitive layout with a clear scrolling structure, allowing easy navigation to different sections.
  • Responsive Design: It performs well across various devices, adapting the layout to fit desktops, tablets, and smartphones, providing a seamless experience regardless of screen size.
  • Engaging Content: The storytelling approach connects users with the brand, offering well-written content about the benefits of yerba mate and the company's philosophy.
  • Fast Loading Speed: Pages load quickly, which demonstrates good technical optimization.

RESN

Resn is a digital design agency known for creating immersive and interactive web experiences. Their website showcases their expertise through creative animations, cutting-edge visuals, and innovative navigation.

Reasons why I like the website:

  • Aesthetic Appeal: The site features a visually dynamic and modern design, using bold colors, interactive elements, and creative animations that capture the attention of visitors.
  • Showcase of Work: The portfolio section effectively displays the agency’s projects in an engaging way, using interactive previews and case studies to demonstrate expertise.
  • Easter Eggs: The website is filled with hidden surprises and playful "easter eggs" that add an extra layer of fun and engagement for users.

LUSION

Lusion is a cutting-edge digital design agency that specializes in creating immersive and visually stunning web experiences.

Reasons why I like the website:

  • Immersive Animations: Lusion utilizes immersive animations that enhance user engagement, providing a dynamic feel that captures the essence of their creative work.
  • Hover Animations: Subtle effects that engage users as they navigate the site.
  • Easter Eggs: The website is filled with hidden surprises and playful "easter eggs" that add an extra layer of fun and engagement for users.

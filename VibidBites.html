Vibid Bites

In this photography project, I explore the vibrant world of food through the lens of contrasting colors. I aim to highlight not only the visual appeal of the food but also its ability to evoke emotions and sensations.

Each image captures the interplay between hues, textures, and forms, inviting viewers to appreciate the artistry of ingredients while celebrating the beauty found in vibrant contrasts. Some of this pictures were used for the project "The Art of Edible Contrast".
